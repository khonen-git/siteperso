# Contract types

<MdxCard>
  A **contract type** describes *how* you get exposure to an underlying (equities, FX, crypto, etc.): cash, term commitment, leverage, optionality.  
  [Asset classes](/knowledge/finance/asset-classes) cover the **what**; this page covers the **how**.
</MdxCard>

        <TableCell className="font-medium">Spot (cash)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Equities, FX, crypto, commodities (physical rare)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Immediate buy/sell; ownership or delivery per market rules</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Underlying price, fees, dividends / crypto spot carry</TableCell>
        <TableCell>No native leverage; capital tied up; slippage on large tickets</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Forward / NDF</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Mostly FX; sometimes commodities</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Commitment to a price at maturity; NDF = cash settlement in currency</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Forward points (rate differentials), expiry date</TableCell>
        <TableCell>OTC counterparty risk; no standardized daily MTM in all cases</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Dated future</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Indices, rates, commodities, some crypto (CME…)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Standardized contract, fixed expiry; initial and variation margin</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Futures price move, **basis** vs spot, **roll** before expiry</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Leverage via margin; liquidity often on front month</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Perpetual (perp)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Mostly crypto; some CFD-like products elsewhere</TableCell>
        <TableCell>No expiry; position can stay open indefinitely</TableCell>
        <TableCell>**Funding rate** (long/short exchange), price vs spot index</TableCell>
        <TableCell>High leverage possible; auto-liquidation if margin fails</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">CFD</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Equities, indices, FX, commodities, crypto (broker-dependent)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Contract with broker: price difference, no underlying delivery</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Broker quote, spreads, overnight swaps, dividend policy</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Counterparty = broker; regulation varies; often restricted for US persons</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Option (vanilla)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Any liquid underlying</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Right (call/put), not obligation; premium paid or received</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Delta, gamma, **theta**, **vega**; vol skew and term structure</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Time decay; implied vol ≠ realized — see Options page</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Swap (rates / TRS)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Rates, credit; sometimes equities (total return swap)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Exchange of cash flows (fixed vs floating, or total return vs funding)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Fixed vs floating leg, spread, duration; TRS: dividend + funding</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Institutional OTC; little pure retail outside CFD proxies</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">ETF</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Equity/bond/commodity baskets, thematic sleeves</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Listed share; fund holding (or replicating) an index/strategy</TableCell>
        <TableCell>NAV vs market price (**premium/discount**), tracking error, TER fees</TableCell>
        <TableCell>ETF liquidity ≠ underlying liquidity; leveraged ETFs (2x/3x) exist</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
    </TableBody>
  </Table>
  </InfoTooltipProvider>

## Spot vs derivative (quick reminder)

<MdxCard>
  <div className="space-y-2">
    <ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-1">
      <li><strong>Spot</strong>: direct exposure, no implicit roll date.</li>
      <li><strong>Linear derivative</strong> (future, perp, CFD): P&amp;L ~ linear in underlying + funding, basis, roll effects.</li>
      <li><strong>Option</strong>: non-linear exposure; asymmetric payoff possible.</li>
