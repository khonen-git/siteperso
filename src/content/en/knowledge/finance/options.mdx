# Options

<MdxCard>
  An **option** grants the **right** (not the obligation) to buy (**call**) or sell (**put**) an underlying at a **strike** price before or at **expiry**.  
  You pay or collect **premium**; P&amp;L depends on the underlying, time, and **implied volatility** — see [Contract types](/knowledge/finance/contract-types) for context, then [Black-Scholes](/knowledge/finance/black-scholes) and [Volatility](/knowledge/finance/volatility) for valuation.
</MdxCard>

## Common strategies

            </InfoTooltip>
          </span>
        </TableHead>
      </TableRow>
    </TableHeader>
    <TableBody>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Covered call</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Long underlying + short call</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ+, Γ−, Θ+, ν−</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Premium income; upside capped above strike</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Income in sideways markets; give up extreme upside</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Protective put</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Long underlying + long put</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ+, Γ+, Θ−, ν+</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Loss floored below strike (minus premium); upside retained</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Ongoing insurance cost if rolled</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Bull call spread</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Long lower-strike call + short higher-strike call</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ+, Γ≈0, Θ−, ν+ (low)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Capped gain; loss limited to net debit</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Cheaper than naked call</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Bear put spread</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Long higher-strike put + short lower-strike put</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ−, Γ≈0, Θ−, ν+ (low)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Capped gain; limited loss</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Logical mirror of bull call spread</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Long straddle</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Long call + long put (same strike, same expiry)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ≈0, Γ+, Θ−, ν+</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Loss if S near strike; profit if move is large enough</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Long volatility; time decay headwind</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Long strangle</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Long OTM call + long OTM put</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ≈0, Γ+, Θ−, ν+</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Wider loss zone; needs a bigger move</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Often before earnings / binary events</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Short straddle</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Short call + short put (same strike)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ≈0, Γ−, Θ+, ν−</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Max profit if S = strike; unlimited risk on large moves</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Experienced traders only; high margin</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Iron condor</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Put spread + call spread (OTM strikes)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ≈0, Γ−, Θ+, ν−</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Net credit if S between wings; loss capped</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Popular short-vol structure; watch tails</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Iron butterfly</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Short straddle + OTM wings</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ≈0, Γ−, Θ+, ν−</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Max profit if S = center strike; wings cap risk</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Condor « pinched » around ATM strike</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
      <TableRow>
        <TableCell className="font-medium">Calendar spread</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Long far expiry + short near expiry (same strike)</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Δ≈0, Γ±, Θ±, ν+</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Sensitive to vol term structure</TableCell>
        <TableCell>Exposure highly maturity-dependent; validate signs per trade</TableCell>
      </TableRow>
    </TableBody>
  </Table>

## Practical reminders

<MdxCard>
  <div className="space-y-2">
    <ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-1">
      <li><strong>ITM / ATM / OTM</strong>: in-the-money, at-the-money, out-of-the-money vs spot and strike.</li>
      <li><strong>American vs European style</strong> (option contract terms, not geography): <em>American</em> = exercise allowed before expiry; <em>European</em> = exercise only at expiry. E.g. many US single-stock options are American-style; many index options (SPX, Euro Stoxx…) are European-style — including on US underlyings.</li>
      <li><strong>Contract multiplier</strong>: US equity options often × 100; check specs per market.</li>
    </ul>
