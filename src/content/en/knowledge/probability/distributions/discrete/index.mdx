# Discrete distributions

<MdxCard>
**Discrete laws** model random variables on finite or countable support. The three fundamental laws below form a chain: one trial ([Bernoulli](/knowledge/probability/distributions/discrete/bernoulli)), the sum of <MathInline>n</MathInline> trials ([binomial](/knowledge/probability/distributions/discrete/binomial)), then the rare-counting limit ([Poisson](/knowledge/probability/distributions/discrete/poisson)).
</MdxCard>
